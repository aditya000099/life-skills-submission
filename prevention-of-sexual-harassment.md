# Prevention of Sexual Harassment (PoSH)

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

Any behavior that causes someone to feel uncomfortable, unsafe, or disrespected is considered sexual harassment. It might be verbal, visual, or physical. Online acts having same effects are also considered sexual harassment.

### Examples include:

* Bodyshaming or dirty jokes
* Sending inappropriate messages or media.
* Touching inappropriately.
* Staring at someone that makes the other person uncomfortable.

## 2. What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

If I face sexual harassment:

* I will ask the person to stop.
* I’ll collect proofs to present them in legal way.
* I will report it at the dedicated place.
* I will be ready for self defence if required.

### If I see someone being harassed:

* I will encourage them to report it.
* If it’s serious, I’ll report it myself.
