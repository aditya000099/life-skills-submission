# Grit and Growth Mindset

Grit is the passion and perseverance we have toward long term goals. It means staying committed and motivated, even when things become difficult. Higher the grit a person has, lower are his chances of quitting before accomplishing his long term goals.

### Growth Mindset

Growth mindset means believing in self that we can do anything that we want. It also means finding our behavioral patterns so we know ourselves better and program our brain into believing that we can achieve anything that we think. A person with a growth mindset believes that skills are built through practice, not god gifted.

### Internal locus of control

Internal locus of control means believing that our success and failure depends upon our own efforts and actions, not on luck.
From the video - To stay motivated, we should focus on things which we can control, such as our efforts, habits, and attitude.

### How to build a growth mindset

- Believe in practice
- Find positivity in every situation
- Take bigger challenges and try your best
- Push yourself out of comfort zone
- Always believe current position is not final position
- Identify weaknesses and work upon them

### My actions for building my growth mindset as a software developer

* Practice and apply what I learn
* Keeping myself updated on latest technical advancements
* Set achievable monthly and yearly goals and track my progress
* Maintain daily consistency in my work
