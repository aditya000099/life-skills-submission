# Learning Process Reflection

## 1. **What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.**

The Feynman Technique is a method of learning where you understand a concept deeply by explaining it in simple words as if teaching a child.

## 2. **In the TED talk, what was the most interesting story or idea for you?**

1. The most interesting idea for me was that anyone can learn difficult subjects by changing how they think.
2. Barbara Oakley shared how she struggled with math but later became an engineer.
3. This showed me that intelligence is not fixed.
4. With the right method and practice, we can improve.

## 3. **What are active and diffused modes of thinking?**

1. Active mode is when we focus deeply on a problem and try to solve it step by step.
2. It is used when studying or practicing questions.
3. Diffused mode is when we relax and let the brain connect ideas in the background.
4. It happens during walks, sleep, or breaks.
5. Both modes are important for learning properly.

## 4. **According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? (Only points)**
